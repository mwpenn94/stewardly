# Research Notes: The Rise of AI Agents in 2026

## Market size
- Global AI agents market: **$10.91B in 2026**, up from $7.63B in 2025 (~43% YoY). [Grand View Research via Ringly roundup, Apr 2026]
- Projected to hit **$50.31B by 2030** at 45.8% CAGR. [Grand View Research]
- Fortune Business Insights: agentic AI grows from **$7.29B (2025) → $139.19B (2034)** at 40.5% CAGR.
- BCC Research: AI agents market $5.7B → $48.3B by 2030 at 43.3% CAGR.
- Precedence Research: real-time decision-making agents CAGR ~43.97% from 2026.
- Multi-agent system platforms projected **$391.94B by 2035** (Precedence).
- Enterprise agentic slice: $2.58B (2024) → $24.50B (2030), 46.2% CAGR (GVR).

## Adoption
- **51%** of enterprises have AI agents in production in 2026; another 23% scaling. (G2 via OneReach.ai)
- **85%** of enterprises have deployed or plan to deploy AI agents by end of 2026.
- **88%** of orgs use AI regularly in at least one business function (McKinsey State of AI 2025, up from 78%).
- **62%** experimenting with AI agents; **<10%** scaling them in production.
- **99%** of enterprise AI developers are exploring or building AI agents (IBM Think / Morning Consult, n=1,000).
- Databricks: only **19%** of orgs have deployed AI agents, but those deployments generate **97%** of new databases.
- Multi-agent systems grew **327%** in under 4 months. (Databricks State of AI Agents 2026)
- **81%** of teams have deployed AI agents; only **14%** have security approval. (cited Mar 2026)
- **49.7%** of all AI agent activity is in software engineering. (LinkedIn analysis, Apr 2026)

## Gartner predictions
- **40%** of enterprise apps will embed task-specific AI agents by end of 2026 (from <5% in 2025).
- **>40%** of agentic AI projects will be canceled by end of 2027 (cost, unclear value, weak risk controls).
- **33%** of enterprise apps will include agentic AI by 2028 (from <1% in 2024).
- **70%** of enterprises will use agentic AI in IT ops by 2029.
- **15%** of daily work decisions autonomous by 2028 (from 0% in 2024).
- Agentic AI could drive **~30% of enterprise software revenue by 2035** (~$450B, from 2% in 2025).

## Productivity / ROI
- Two-thirds of EMEA enterprises report significant productivity gains from AI; 24% of those say AI "fundamentally changed" operations. (IBM, Oct 2025)
- PwC: **88%** of senior execs plan to increase AI budgets in next 12 months due to agentic AI.
- KPMG Q1 2026 Pulse: US enterprises project **avg $207M** AI spending over next 12 months, ~2x prior year.
- Conversational AI projected to save **$80B** in contact-center costs by 2026.
- Linde audit case: **92%** reduction in audit-report preparation time (MIT HDSR, 2026).

## Department split (for ops functions)
- **60%** deploying agents in operations; **40%** in marketing/sales; customer service heavy usage. (Techstrong, Nov 2025)

## Challenges
- **79%** of orgs face challenges adopting AI (up double digits from 2025); 54% of C-suite... (Writer.com, Apr 2026)
- Forrester 2025: agents "fail in unexpected and costly ways" in enterprise deployment.
- Security lag: 81% deployed, only 14% security-approved.
